The problem we solve

The Canada → Nigeria vehicle import corridor is characterised by fraud, opaque landed costs and customs uncertainty. Dealers carry high financial risk on every shipment.

VIN fraud & misrepresentation

Cloned or invalid VINs, salvage histories and incomplete disclosure make it difficult to know what is really being purchased.

Unpredictable landed cost

Duties, levies, port charges and FX volatility mean the final cost often diverges significantly from initial expectations.

Customs delays & penalties

Missing or inconsistent documentation leads to valuation disputes, storage fees and delayed delivery to end-buyers.

Who we serve

Auto-Bridge is designed for high-risk, high-volume import decision makers on the Canada → Nigeria lane.

Independent auto dealers

Importing 100–300 vehicles per year from North America and seeking predictable, verifiable offers with reliable clearance outcomes.

Fleet operators

Ride-hailing and logistics fleets requiring mechanically sound, low-maintenance vehicles and transparent total cost of ownership.

Diaspora shippers (roadmap)

Nigerians in Canada shipping vehicles and personal cargo to Lagos with predictable cost, documentation handled and minimal last-mile friction.

How Auto-Bridge works

A single workflow connecting Canadian sellers, VIN data, inspections, logistics and Nigerian Customs.

1

Select & verify vehicle

Auto-Bridge connects to Canadian sellers and auctions and performs VIN history checks. Certified inspectors confirm actual condition on site before funds are committed.

2

See true landed cost

Our cost-intelligence engine combines FOB price, freight, duties, levies, port charges and clearing rules to provide a pre-purchase landed-cost estimate.

3

Book logistics & documentation

Auto-Bridge coordinates trucking, ocean shipping and pre-files Customs documentation via vetted Nigeria Customs brokers and shipping lines.

4

Track until delivery

Dealers monitor every stage from pickup in Canada to discharge at Apapa or Tin Can, with exceptions and delays flagged in the platform.
