The problem we solve

The Canada → Nigeria import corridor is characterised by fraud, opaque landed costs and customs uncertainty. Dealers currently carry significant financial risk on every shipment.

VIN fraud & misrepresentation

Cloned or invalid VINs, incomplete history and salvage titles make it difficult to know what is truly being purchased from overseas sellers or auctions.

Unpredictable landed cost

Duties, levies, port charges and FX movements mean the final cost of a vehicle can diverge sharply from expectations — eroding margins and trust.

Customs delays & penalties

Missing or inconsistent documentation leads to valuation disputes, storage fees and delayed delivery to end-customers.

How Auto-Bridge works

A structured workflow that links Canadian sellers, VIN data, inspections, logistics and Nigerian Customs into one transparent process.

1

Select & verify vehicle

Auto-Bridge connects to Canadian sellers and auctions and runs VIN history checks. Certified inspectors perform on-site visual and mechanical checks before funds are committed.

2

See true landed cost

Our cost-intelligence engine combines FOB value, freight, duties, levies, port and clearing fees to generate a pre-purchase landed-cost estimate.

3

Book logistics & documentation

Auto-Bridge coordinates trucking to port, ocean shipping and pre-files documentation with vetted Nigeria Customs brokers and shipping lines.

4

Track until delivery

Dealers can monitor each stage — from pickup in Canada through port discharge and clearance at Apapa or Tin Can — with delays or exceptions highlighted in the platform.
